Who We Are

Ever thought of speaking [wanted to speak] to your phone in Igbo ? Ever thought of saying [wanted to say] Kedu Siri, biko nye m aka kpo nne’m ?…well unfortunately you couldn’t (for now).

Those technologies do not support only a handful of languages with Igbo obviously not being a part of those despite being spoken by close to 30 million people.

Igwebuike, which means "Unity is Strength", is a grassroots community whose vision is to:

  • Initiate a unified and sustained global effort towards the preservation of the Igbo Language
  • Curate Igbo speech data that will enable the development of various speech-related Artificial intelligence interfaces/platforms, using the in Igbo Language
  • Entrench Igbo Language as one of the dominant technological languages of the world
  • Create gateways for the development of varieties of Igbo-speech-related artificial intelligence technologies and interfaces

Our Mission

  • Creating a global community of Igbo language speakers, who will volunteer to assist in realizing our stated target of recording 10,000 voices in Igbo Language
  • Achieving our set target of 1000 hours of recorded and validated Igbo sentences

How to Contribute

  1. Recording your voice here
  2. Share with your friends. Encourage them to record
  3. Reach out to communities and spread the word
  4. You can join our Slack or Telegram Group
